Coventry & North Warwickshire Cricket Club

Welcome to Coventry & North Warwickshire Sports Club, first established in 1851. We boast some of the best sport, leisure and social facilities in the county and is a true hidden treasure.

Our Teams includes...

Junior Section

And Senior Section


Junior Sides

Junior teams start with the under-9s, and continue up to the under-17s. Our Junior section is incredibly popular for both boys and girls, and as well as coaching, teams and leagues, we also organise school holiday camps which include a visit to a live cricket match.

We have some great young players with a bright sporting future ahead of them, and some of our former juniors have gone on to incredible success - including Warwickshire and England batsman Ian Bell


Senior Sides

We have a range of teams competing in local leagues, from our 1st XI to the 4th XI, and we also have popular Sunday and Midweek teams.

Membership

World class champions have started at our club but the majority of our members join to keep healthy, improve fitness, have fun and compete in their choice of sport. Cricket, Tennis, Netball, Bridge, Squash, Racquetball and Gym members all share our club house, where they can enjoy a drink or meal with friends and relax in a comfortable environment. We also have many members who join the club for the social events and friendly atmosphere. Our newly refurbished Ian Bell Sports Bar stocks a wide range of drinks and snacks, as well as a full-size snooker table and a pool table. We also have Sky and BT Sport, so members can enjoy watching any sporting event of their choice on our wide screen televisions.

Facilities

From cricket pitches and squash, netball and tennis courts, to our function rooms, bar and gym, Coventry & North Warwickshire Sports Club has everything you need for your sporting and social life!
